Frequently asked questions
What is Interview Intelligence?

Interview Intelligence refers to the use of AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze and score candidate responses in a video interview.

How does Interview Intelligence benefit the hiring process?

Interview Intelligence helps to reduce bias, save time, and provide a more objective evaluation of candidate responses. This leads to a more efficient and effective hiring process.

What is the Interview Structuring Tool?

The Interview Structuring Tool helps hiring managers provide guidelines and instructions to interviewers during the interview process. It helps ensure a consistent and structured interview experience for candidates.

How does the Interview Structuring Tool improve the interview process?

The Interview Structuring Tool ensures that all interviews are conducted in a consistent manner and eliminates the potential for interviewers to stray from the intended questions and topics. This leads to a more fair and impartial interview process.

Can the Interview Structuring Tool be customized for my company's needs?

Yes, the Interview Structuring Tool can be tailored to meet the specific needs of your company and the role being interviewed.

How does BarRaiser's video interview tool compare to other interview tools on the market?

BarRaiser's video interview tool is unique in its use of AI and machine learning algorithms to analyze and score candidate's Interviews, as well as its Interview Structuring Tool. These features set it apart from other interview tools and offer a more efficient and effective solution for the recruitment process.
